Our Story

Two Empires. One Vision.

AustroIndie was born from the friendship of two artisan families — one from the gilded halls of Vienna, the other from the vibrant ateliers of Jaipur.

United by a shared reverence for craftsmanship, we bring you pieces that carry the soul of both imperial courts: the precision of Habsburg goldsmiths and the opulence of Mughal weavers. Every thread, every stone, every brushstroke in our collection tells a story of two worlds meeting in perfect harmony.

Our Journey

The AustroIndie Story

❧

  • 2018

    THE FIRST MEETING

    Two families meet at a global textile fair in Vienna — a Jaipur weaving family and a Viennese goldsmith clan discover their shared passion for heritage craftsmanship.

  • 2020

    THE VISION FORMS

    During the global pause, the two families collaborate remotely — exchanging sketches, fabrics, and gold samples across continents, refining a unique fusion aesthetic.

  • 2022

    FIRST COLLECTION

    The first AustroIndie collection launches in Jaipur — 12 pieces blending Mughal motifs with Habsburg-era filigree techniques. Sells out within a week.

  • 2025

    THE DIGITAL PALACE

    AustroIndie opens its digital boutique — bringing the finest sarees, imitation jewellery, and decorative items to discerning patrons worldwide.
